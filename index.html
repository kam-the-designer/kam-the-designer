About Me

Kamleshwar Sharma

The creative force behind Kam The Designer

I'm Kamleshwar Sharma, a graphic design professional with over 12 years of experience in turning ideas into meaningful visuals. I don't just design — I build identities that speak, sell, and stick.

From startups to established brands, I've partnered with businesses across industries — real estate, fashion, beauty, retail, and more — helping them communicate with clarity and impact. Whether it's a sleek social media post, a bold logo, or eye-catching packaging, every design I create is rooted in strategy and shaped by creativity.

I'm fluent in the industry's top tools — Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, CorelDRAW, Canva & Figma — delivering tailor-made solutions that are not only beautiful but purposeful.
